### Business Model Differences

**Mining (Pertambangan):**
- Revenue = gold price × production volume
- Profitability driven by gold price movements
- Key metric: cash cost (AISC)
- Products: gold ore → doré (10-30% gold content)

**Trading/Refinery (Perdagangan):**
- Revenue from spread (buy-sell margin)
- Profitability driven by volume, not gold price
- Margin relatively stable at 4-5%
- Products: bullion (99.9%), minted bars, jewelry

### Gold Industry Chain
1. Exploration — find reserves, assess economic viability
2. Mining — open pit or underground, produce doré
3. Refinery — purify doré → bullion (99.5-99.9% Au)
4. Fabrication — bullion → minted bars, jewelry
5. Financial services — gadai emas (pawning), tabungan emas (savings), cicil emas (installments) — BRIS, Pegadaian

### Technical Details
- Economic gold grade: 0.5-5 g/t Au
- Oxide ore: near surface, lower grade, open pit, heap leach
- Sulfide ore: underground, higher grade, CIL processing
- JORC standard for reserves/resources reporting
- Heap leach: low cost, lower recovery, for <1 g/t ore
- Carbon-in-Leach (CIL): higher cost, better recovery, for >1 g/t ore

### Royalty Changes
- 2024: PP 26/2022 — 10% royalty (gold ≥$2,000/oz)
- 2025: PP 18/2025 — 16% royalty (gold ≥$3,000/oz)

### Why Gold Prices Up +61% YTD
- Fed rate cut expectations
- De-dollarization trend
- Geopolitical/macro uncertainty

### Indonesia-Specific Facts
- Copper mines produce gold as by-product (porphyry copper-gold deposits)
- Gold mines DON'T produce copper (epithermal gold deposits — copper not economic to extract)
- Only Grasberg has transitioned from open pit to underground
- ANTM's refinery is the ONLY LBMA-accredited in Indonesia
