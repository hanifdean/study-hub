ğŸ¯ HBM: Why It's Special

HBM = High Bandwidth Memory

It's still DRAM, but stacked vertically and connected directly to the GPU via silicon interposer. This is the critical bottleneck for AI.

Regular DRAM HBM
Bandwidth ~50 GB/s 1,000+ GB/s
Form factor Separate chip on motherboard Stacked, on-package with GPU
Manufacturing Standard process Advanced 3D stacking + interposer
Why AI needs it Can't keep up with modern GPUs Only HBM can feed massive AI models fast enough
