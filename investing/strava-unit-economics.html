ARPU (Average Revenue Per User) ‚Ü© back

Total revenue divided by number of users. Measures how much revenue each user generates on average.

LTV (Lifetime Value) ‚Ü© back

The total revenue a company expects from a single customer over their entire relationship. Higher LTV = more valuable customers.

CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) ‚Ü© back

The cost of acquiring one new customer, including marketing and sales expenses. Healthy businesses have LTV >> CAC.

Churn Rate ‚Ü© back

The percentage of subscribers who cancel in a given period. Lower churn = stickier product = more predictable revenue.

ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue) ‚Ü© back

The annualized value of recurring subscription revenue. The key metric for SaaS/subscription businesses.

Gross Margin ‚Ü© back

Revenue minus cost of goods sold, as a percentage of revenue. Shows how much profit is left after direct costs.
