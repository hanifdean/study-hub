US-China Convergence: The Superpower Trade

Why the US and China will become more like each other ‚Äî and what that means for investors.

üê± Robin's TLDR

The irony of US-China decoupling is that each must borrow from their rival to stay competitive. The US must reinvest in infrastructure and manufacturing (become more like China). China must liberalize and stimulate consumption (become more like US). Because of their different starting points, this convergence favors Chinese equities over the next 5 years. The RMB is severely undervalued, Chinese equities trade at a 5x discount to US by % of global markets, and the gap is likely to close.
