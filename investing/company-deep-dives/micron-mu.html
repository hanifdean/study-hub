üè¢ Company Overview

Micron Technology is the only U.S.-headquartered memory and storage manufacturer, founded in 1978 and based in Boise, Idaho. It employs ~53,000 people with fabs across the U.S. (Idaho, Virginia, New York), Japan, Singapore, Taiwan, China, Malaysia, and India. Micron designs and sells DRAM, NAND, HBM, and NOR flash. The Cloud Memory Business Unit (HBM + server DRAM) has become the single largest and highest-margin segment, reflecting the AI infrastructure shift.

Moats & Strategic Advantages

Oligopoly pricing discipline: 3 players control >90% of DRAM; capacity cuts after 2022‚Äì23 downcycle are holding.
Technology leadership: 1Œ≥ DRAM node delivers ~15% power advantage; majority of output by H2 CY2026.
HBM execution: #2 player, strong yields, full 2026 allocation.
Only U.S. supplier: CHIPS Act funding and geopolitical preference for domestic memory.
Vertical integration: Wafer-to-module control enables faster HBM and LP-SOCAMM2 ramp.
G9 NAND platform: Enables 122TB and 245TB QLC SSDs for hyperscalers.

‚ö†Ô∏è Risks

Cyclicality: Memory is boom-bust. FY26 CapEx ~$20B increases downside if demand slows. Counter: HBM structurally tightens DRAM supply; AI demand less volatile than PCs.
China restrictions: Micron exited China server chip market (TrendForce, Oct 2025). Counter: U.S. government/defense and hyperscalers prioritize domestic supply.
Samsung HBM4 comeback: If Samsung fixes yields, HBM ASPs could compress. Counter: Micron already fully allocated for 2026 and targets 30% share.
Capital intensity: $20B FY26 CapEx + Singapore $24B fab + $7B HBM packaging plant. Counter: Q1 FY26 OCF $8.4B shows funding capacity.
Tariff uncertainty: 2026 trade policy could disrupt Asian supply chains. Counter: Expanded U.S. manufacturing footprint reduces exposure.
Customer concentration: Hyperscalers + NVIDIA ecosystem dominate revenue. Counter: Multi-year AI buildout with committed HBM supply contracts.
Valuation premium: All-time high price; any guide cut could cause sharp drawdown. Counter: Forward P/E ~13x still modest for 50%+ growth.
