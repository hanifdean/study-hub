üß± The Copper Wall ‚Äî Why Optics is Inevitable

AI clusters now exceed 1M GPUs. Data movement, not compute, is the bottleneck.
Copper fails at high speeds. At 1.6T, copper requires ~15 pJ/bit and burns more power as heat than signal.
SerDes scaling breaks. Power per lane rises faster than bandwidth, accelerating the copper wall.
Optics saves power. SiPh + CPO reduce energy to 5‚Äì6 pJ/bit (~70% reduction), cutting total networking power ~50%.
Nomura forecast. 1.6T optical modules ship 2.5M (2025) ‚Üí 20M (2026), an 8x jump.
SiPh penetration. 50‚Äì70% share in the highest-end 1.6T segment.
NVIDIA proof point. Spectrum-X Ethernet Photonics cuts power per port ~5x.
This is survival, not optional. AI data centers cannot scale without optics.

üè¢ Company Overview

Lumentum is headquartered in San Jose, California and was spun out of JDS Uniphase in 2015. It is the market leader in EML lasers and a critical supplier of optical components for hyperscale AI data centers. Two operating segments drive the business: Cloud & Networking (~88% of revenue) and Industrial Tech (~12%).
