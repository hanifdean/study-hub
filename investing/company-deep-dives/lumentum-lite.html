üöÄ Growth Drivers

AI Data Center Interconnects ‚Äî Optical Scale-Out

Architectural shift: Copper scale-up ‚Üí optical scale-out is irreversible in AI clusters.
Capex tailwind: Hyperscalers spending $50B+ annually; ~10% flows to networking/optics.
Mix shift: Cloud/AI now >60% of Lumentum revenue, up from ~40% two years ago.

800G ‚Üí 1.6T Ramp (Core Driver)

800G volume driver: Today‚Äôs standard using 100G/lane EML chips.
1.6T forecast: ~35M units in 2026 (doubled vs early estimates).
200G/lane EML: Lumentum shipping substantial orders; first fills expected.
Content per module: Each speed generation raises optical content and margins.

Co-Packaged Optics (CPO)

Largest purchase commitment in company history for ultra-high-power lasers.
CPO ramp: Expected in CY H2 2026 as optical engines move onto switch ASICs.
Specialty lasers: Lumentum‚Äôs CPO requires high-power CW lasers.

Optical Circuit Switches (OCS)

First revenue: Q4 FY25; shipping to two customers, third committed for CY2026.
High-ASP category: Reconfigurable optical networking inside AI clusters.
Optionality: Potential $500M+ line by FY27+ if adoption accelerates.

Narrow-Linewidth Lasers for DCI

Demand outpacing supply: Six consecutive quarters of growth.
DCI tailwind: DCI builds require ZR/ZR+ coherent pluggables.

Capacity Expansion

InP fab +40%: San Jose capacity expansion on schedule.
Fabrinet manufacturing: Scales transceiver output in Thailand.
In-house OCS production: Accelerating into 2026.

Valuation priced to perfection: +340% in 2025, +750% from lows. Counter: structural optical demand shift and supply constraints support premium.

üìñ Jargon Decoder

EML (Electro-Absorption Modulated Laser) ‚Ü© back
Laser + modulator on one chip; the gold standard for 100G/200G-per-lane datacom.
InP (Indium Phosphide) ‚Ü© back
Semiconductor substrate used for high-speed lasers with superior optical performance.
CPO (Co-Packaged Optics) ‚Ü© back
Integrates optical engines with switch ASICs to reduce power and latency.
OCS (Optical Circuit Switch) ‚Ü© back
Reconfigurable optical switching fabric inside AI clusters.
DCI (Data Center Interconnect) ‚Ü© back
High-capacity links connecting data centers across metro/long-haul distances.
Transceiver ‚Ü© back
Module that converts electrical signals to optical and back; the core of networking links.
ZR / ZR+ ‚Ü© back
Coherent pluggable optics standards for 400G/800G long-haul DCI.
VCSEL ‚Ü© back
Vertical-cavity surface-emitting laser used in 3D sensing (e.g., Face ID).
800G ‚Ü© back
800 gigabit-per-second optical module standard; current volume node.
1.6T ‚Ü© back
1.6 terabit-per-second transceiver generation; next major upgrade wave.
Silicon Photonics ‚Ü© back
Optical components built on silicon wafers; lower cost but debated at 1.6T+.
