üéØ Sector Investment Thesis

üêÇ Bull Case for Japan Semi

  • Irreplaceable positions: Multiple monopolies (EUV mask, coater, T-glass) with no viable substitutes
  • AI infrastructure picks & shovels: Every NVIDIA GPU requires Japan equipment and materials
  • Government support: Japan investing heavily in domestic semiconductor capacity
  • Yen tailwind potential: Weak yen benefits export-oriented equipment makers
  • Memory supercycle: NAND/DRAM recovery benefits Kioxia, equipment demand

üêª Bear Case / Risks

  • Cyclicality: Semiconductor equipment is historically volatile
  • Premium valuations: Many stocks have 2-5x'd; limited margin for error
  • China exposure: ~20-25% revenue at risk from export controls
  • Yen volatility: Currency moves impact reported earnings significantly
  • Peak cycle risk: AI demand may be approaching near-term saturation
