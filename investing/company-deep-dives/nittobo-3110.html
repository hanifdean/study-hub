üè¢ Company Overview

Nitto Boseki Co., Ltd. (Nittobo) was founded in 1918 as a textile company and evolved into a specialty glass fiber and electronic materials manufacturer. The company developed proprietary NE-glass (now T-glass) technology with low dielectric constant (Low-Dk) and low dielectric dissipation factor, essential for high-frequency signal integrity in advanced semiconductor substrates.

üî¨ Why T-Glass Matters for AI

Signal integrity: Low-Dk glass reduces signal loss at high frequencies (10+ GHz)
High-temp stability: Maintains properties through packaging thermal cycles
ABF substrate critical: T-glass is key input for Ajinomoto Build-up Film substrates
GPU packaging: Every NVIDIA H100/H200/B100 uses ABF substrates with T-glass
Supply shortage: T-glass constrained ‚Üí ABF constrained ‚Üí GPU constrained
AI customers prioritized: Hyperscalers paying premium, starving other segments
No easy substitutes: Standard E-glass lacks required electrical properties
2-year lead time: New capacity takes years to qualify and ramp
