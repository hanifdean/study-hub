üè¢ Company Overview

Alamtri Resources (ADRO) is the post spin-off holding company of the Adaro group. After spinning off the thermal coal business into AADI, ADRO now owns a strategic stake in ADMR (met coal + aluminium) and the Adaro Green platform. Management aims to shift revenue mix to 50% non-coal by 2030 through hilirisasi and renewables.
