üè¢ Company Overview

Advantest Corporation, founded in 1954, is a global leader in semiconductor test systems and measuring instruments. The company provides Automatic Test Equipment (ATE) for testing memory chips (DRAM, NAND, HBM), SoCs, and high-performance computing devices. Advantest's testers are essential for ensuring chip quality before packaging and shipping.
