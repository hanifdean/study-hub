üß≠ Executive Summary

Advanced packaging materials leader. Resonac (formerly Showa Denko) is a critical supplier of materials used in semiconductor back-end packaging, including CoWoS, HBM, and advanced 3D packaging.
TSMC recognition. Received "2025 TSMC Excellent Performance Award" for technological contributions in advanced packaging materials and high-purity gas localization.
JOINT3 consortium. Leading a 27-member consortium to develop next-generation semiconductor packaging technologies, with prototype production starting 2026.
Diversified chemical business. Semiconductor materials are high-growth segment within broader specialty chemicals portfolio.

üè¢ Company Overview

Resonac Holdings Corporation was formed in 2023 through the integration of Showa Denko and Hitachi Chemical. The company is a major specialty chemicals and materials company with strong positions in semiconductor materials, both front-end (wafer processing) and back-end (packaging). Resonac operates the "Packaging Solution Center" with state-of-the-art semiconductor packaging equipment.
