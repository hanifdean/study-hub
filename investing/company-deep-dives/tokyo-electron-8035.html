üè¢ Company Overview

Tokyo Electron Limited (TEL) was founded in 1963 and is headquartered in Tokyo. The company designs, manufactures, and sells semiconductor production equipment globally. TEL's product portfolio spans the entire front-end wafer fabrication process, from deposition to patterning to etch. The company is the critical "picks and shovels" supplier for TSMC, Samsung, Intel, and every major foundry.

  • Competition: Applied Materials and Lam Research competing aggressively in etch/deposition
