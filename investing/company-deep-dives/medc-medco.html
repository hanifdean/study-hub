üè¢ Company Overview

Medco Energi Internasional is an integrated energy company spanning upstream oil & gas, power generation, and a strategic stake in AMMN (Amman Mineral). The core portfolio is gas-heavy, with production from multiple PSC blocks in Indonesia and Oman. Medco Power operates thermal and renewable IPP assets, targeting 4,550 GWh electricity sales with a 24% renewable mix by 2026.
