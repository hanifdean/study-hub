üè¢ Company Overview

SanDisk is the only U.S.-listed pure-play NAND flash and SSD manufacturer, spun off from Western Digital on Feb 24, 2025. HQ in Milpitas, CA with ~11,000 employees under CEO David Goeckeler. The company operates a 25+ year joint venture with Kioxia, giving it access to the Yokkaichi and Kitakami fabs in Japan and the BiCS FLASH roadmap.
