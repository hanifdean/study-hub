üè¢ Company Overview

Elnusa (ELSA) is an Indonesian oil & gas services company and part of the Pertamina ecosystem. Its revenue mix spans upstream services (seismic, drilling, well services), energy distribution & logistics (fuel trading and distribution), and supporting services (IT, fleet, maintenance). The business is designed to be stable rather than cyclical, making ELSA a defensive energy services exposure.

üí° Thesis Snapshot

Defensive energy services play: ELSA is tied to national oil & gas activity and tends to deliver steady revenue even when commodity prices soften.
Pertamina-linked pipeline: Majority ownership and strategic importance support contract visibility and recurring demand.
Balanced segment mix: Upstream activity provides growth when oil & gas capex rises, while distribution/logistics and supporting services stabilize cash flows.
