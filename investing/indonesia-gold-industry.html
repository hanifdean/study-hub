üß™ Technical Deep Dive

Ore Types & Mining

Oxide ore: near surface, lower grade; open pit with heap leach.
Sulfide ore: higher grade, underground, processed via CIL.
Deposits: porphyry copper-gold vs epithermal deposits (gold-only).

Processing & Economics

Grade economics: economic grades typically 0.5‚Äì5 g/t Au.
Recovery: heap leach = lower recovery rate, CIL = higher recovery.
Cost metrics: AISC and cash cost dictate resilience.
Standard: JORC governs resource disclosure.
